## Course Overview

This program is designed for accounting students, international accountants, tax preparers, bookkeepers, and entrepreneurs. It progresses from beginner to expert and prepares students to file complete US individual and basic business tax returns from scratch using professional software.

- **Beginner (Classes 1-5):** Foundation, filing framework, dependency logic
- **Intermediate (Classes 6-12):** Gross income, adjustments, deductions, credits, other taxes
- **Advanced (Classes 13-19):** Capital gains, depreciation, software prep, business returns and K-1
- **Expert (Classes 20-22):** Real-time simulation, compliance mastery, career readiness

## Prerequisites

- No prior US tax preparation background required
- Basic spreadsheet comfort is recommended

## Core Goal

Enable students to confidently prepare:

- Form 1040 (Individual returns)
- Form 1120 (C-Corp)
- Form 1120S (S-Corp)
- Form 1065 (Partnership)

## Delivery Notes

## Grading

- Module quizzes after Modules 2, 4, 5, 6, and 8
- Four progressive return-building assignments
- Final Assessment Part A: 90-minute written exam
- Final Assessment Part B: 3-hour practical return build
