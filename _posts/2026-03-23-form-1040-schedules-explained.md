Form 1040 is not a standalone worksheet. Most real returns rely on schedules for additional income, adjustments, taxes, and credits.

A clean workflow starts by mapping each document to the correct section first, then reconciling totals before final tax calculation.

Our curriculum includes structured drills for Schedules 1, 2, and 3 so learners build speed and accuracy in real preparation work.
