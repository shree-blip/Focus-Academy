Many early-stage mistakes happen when W-2 wages and 1099 income are treated the same way. They are reported differently and often carry different tax implications.

W-2 income usually includes withholding and payroll tax treatment through the employer. 1099 income often requires self-employment tax checks, deduction mapping, and estimated payment awareness.

Our practical classes walk through both forms side by side so learners can classify income correctly before calculating taxable income.
