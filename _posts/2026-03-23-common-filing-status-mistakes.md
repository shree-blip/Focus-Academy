Choosing the correct filing status is one of the most important decisions in an individual tax return. A wrong status can change your tax rate, deduction eligibility, and credit availability.

At Focus Academy, we teach a simple decision process: confirm marital status on the last day of the year, test dependent support rules, then verify household maintenance requirements for Head of Household.

Before filing, always validate your status with supporting facts and documentation. This single check helps avoid many amendment requests and IRS notices.
