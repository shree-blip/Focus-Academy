One of the most frequent exam and client questions is whether to use standard deduction or itemized deduction. The right answer depends on actual eligible expenses and documentation.

A practical approach is to compute both outcomes, compare the taxable income effect, and verify supporting records for Schedule A items.

In class, we use scenario-based worksheets to help learners choose confidently and explain the reasoning to clients.
